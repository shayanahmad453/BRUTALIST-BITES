ABOUT BRUTALIST BITES

OUR STORY

HALAL & NON-HALAL SOURCING

OUR COMMITMENT TO TRANSPARENCY

We understand that dietary requirements are deeply personal and often faith-based. That's why we maintain complete transparency about our sourcing and preparation methods.

HALAL CERTIFIED

All our chicken, lamb, and beef products are certified Halal by the Halal Food Authority (HFA). Our meat is sourced exclusively from suppliers holding current HFA certification.

Certification Number: HFA-UK-2024-8872
Valid Until: December 31, 2025
Certifying Body: Halal Food Authority (HFA)

NON-HALAL ITEMS

We also serve bacon and pork products which are NOT Halal certified. These items are clearly marked on our menu with a icon.

Important: We maintain separate preparation areas and utensils for pork products to prevent cross-contamination. However, our kitchen is NOT a fully segregated Halal-only facility.

MENU LABELING

Every item on our menu is clearly labeled:

  • Halal Certified - Meat from HFA-certified suppliers
  • Contains Pork - Not Halal (bacon, ham, pork)
  • Vegetarian - No meat or fish
  • Vegan - No animal products whatsoever
