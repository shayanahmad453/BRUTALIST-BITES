SUPPORT OUR MISSION

Every donation helps us give back to our community through our "Food for All" program, providing meals to those in need.

WHERE YOUR DONATION GOES

FOOD FOR ALL PROGRAM

We partner with local charities to provide free meals to vulnerable community members. Your donation directly funds:

  • Meals for homeless individuals in Manchester
  • Lunchboxes for underprivileged children
  • Emergency food parcels for families in crisis
  • Community cooking classes teaching nutrition

£10

Provides 5 meals for individuals experiencing homelessness

£25

Feeds a family of 4 for a week with emergency food parcels

£50

Sponsors a cooking class teaching nutrition to 20 people
